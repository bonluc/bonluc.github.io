Crime in San Francisco

This data story explores patterns in crime across San Francisco. Using temporal and spatial visualizations, we uncover when and where crime is most concentrated.

Scroll down to explore different perspectives on the data.

When does crime happen?

This visualization shows how crime varies across the hours of the day. We can observe clear daily patterns, with certain crime types peaking at specific times.

Where does crime concentrate?

Crime is not evenly distributed across the city. This heatmap highlights areas with high crime density, revealing strong spatial clustering.

How does crime evolve over time?

Looking at trends over time allows us to see whether crime is increasing, decreasing, or shifting in nature.

Conclusion

Crime in San Francisco shows clear temporal and spatial patterns. Understanding these patterns can help inform policy decisions and improve public safety strategies.
